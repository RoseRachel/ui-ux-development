Railway sector

The railway sector was introduced in Kenya by the colonialists.The sector is doing well since the it has been upgraded to a more pennan train.In keeping with its mandate, Kenya Railways is committed to providing the country with safe, reliable and efficient railway transport services. The railways master plan sets out to transform the national railway network through the rolling out of the mass commuter rail system for the country's metropolis and the construction of the Standard Gauge Railway (SGR) network.
